What is mNAV?

Modified Net Asset Value (mNAV) measures the premium (or discount) at which a Digital Asset Treasury company trades relative to the Bitcoin it holds.

mNAV = Market Cap ÷ (BTC Holdings x BTC Price)
  • mNAV > 1 → Market is paying a premium over the BTC value
  • mNAV = 1 → Market values MSTR at exactly its BTC holdings
  • mNAV < 1 → Market is discounting the BTC value (rare for MSTR)
